# The Ultimate Guide to Purchase Order Funding in South Africa (2026)

Winning a purchase order is exciting.

Unfortunately, many South African businesses discover that receiving an order and being able to fulfil it are two very different things.

You may have secured a contract with a government department, municipality or private company—but without enough cash to buy stock, pay suppliers or cover operational costs, that opportunity can quickly become a challenge.

This is where **Purchase Order Funding** can make a significant difference.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is purchase order funding a loan?

Not in the traditional sense. It is a transaction-based funding solution linked to a confirmed purchase order.

---

## Can government purchase orders be funded?

Yes. Many providers finance qualifying purchase orders from government departments, municipalities and reputable private companies.

---

## Do I receive the money directly?

In many cases, funding providers pay suppliers directly rather than transferring cash into your business account.

---

## How long does approval take?

Approval times vary depending on the provider, the documentation supplied and how quickly the purchase order can be verified.

---

## Can new businesses qualify?

Yes. New businesses may qualify if they have a genuine purchase order, reliable suppliers and the ability to deliver successfully.
