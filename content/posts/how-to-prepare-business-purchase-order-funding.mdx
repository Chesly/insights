# How to Prepare Your Business Before Applying for Purchase Order Funding

Purchase order funding can help your business fulfil larger contracts without using your own cash.

However, approval doesn't happen simply because you have a purchase order.

Funding providers want confidence that your business can successfully complete the work and that the transaction presents an acceptable level of risk.

Preparing your business before applying can save time, reduce delays and improve your chances of approval.

---

# 1. Verify Your Purchase Order

Before approaching a funding provider, confirm that your purchase order is:

- Genuine
- Signed where required
- Complete
- Issued by an authorised buyer
- Clear about quantities, pricing and delivery dates

If anything appears unclear, resolve it before applying.

---

# 2. Know Your Costs

Many businesses underestimate the true cost of fulfilling an order.

Prepare a detailed costing that includes:

- Stock or materials
- Labour
- Transport
- Packaging
- Insurance
- Unexpected expenses
- Funding costs

A clear costing demonstrates that you understand your business.

---

# 3. Choose Reliable Suppliers

Funding providers often pay suppliers directly.

Using suppliers with a proven track record makes the transaction less risky.

Keep supplier quotations ready and ensure pricing is current.

---

# 4. Organise Your Business Documents

Having documents ready speeds up the approval process.

Typical documents include:

- Company registration documents
- Tax compliance information
- Bank statements
- Director identification
- Supplier quotations
- Purchase order
- Customer details

Different providers may ask for additional information.

---

# 5. Understand Your Customer

Funding providers assess your customer as carefully as they assess your business.

If your customer has a good payment history and is financially stable, approval is generally easier.

---

# 6. Maintain Accurate Financial Records

Even if the purchase order is strong, poor bookkeeping creates unnecessary risk.

Maintain organised records of:

- Income
- Expenses
- Previous contracts
- Banking transactions

Professional financial records build confidence.

---

# 7. Communicate Clearly

Respond promptly to requests for information.

Delays often occur because applications are incomplete or additional documents are submitted late.

Good communication reflects good business management.

---

# Purchase Order Funding Readiness Checklist

Before applying, ask yourself:

✅ Is my purchase order genuine?

✅ Have I calculated all project costs?

✅ Are my suppliers reliable?

✅ Are my documents organised?

✅ Am I tax compliant?

✅ Can my business deliver on time?

✅ Do I understand the funding costs?

If you answered "No" to any of these questions, resolve those issues before applying.

---

# Final Thoughts

Preparation is one of the biggest factors influencing funding success.

Businesses that understand their costs, organise their documents and work with reliable suppliers often move through the approval process more efficiently.

Purchase order funding should support business growth—not create unnecessary stress.

The better prepared you are, the greater your chances of turning an approved purchase order into a successful project.
