# How Long Does Purchase Order Funding Take in South Africa?

One of the biggest concerns for business owners is whether funding will arrive in time to fulfil a purchase order.

The good news is that Purchase Order Funding is generally much faster than applying for a traditional business loan. However, the timeline depends on how prepared your business is and how quickly all parties respond.

---

## Typical Approval Timeline

Although every funding provider has its own process, a straightforward application with complete documentation may follow a timeline like this:

- Application submitted
- Initial review within 24–48 hours
- Verification of the purchase order and customer
- Supplier verification
- Funding agreement issued
- Supplier payment after agreements are signed

For many first-time applications, the full process may take between **5 and 10 working days**, while repeat customers with established relationships are often funded faster. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## What Can Delay Approval?

Common delays include:

- Missing documents
- Incorrect supplier quotations
- SARS compliance issues
- Customer verification delays
- Unclear purchase orders
- Changes to the contract

Many delays are avoidable by preparing your paperwork before applying.

---

## How to Speed Up Your Application

You can improve your turnaround time by:

- Keeping your company documents updated.
- Maintaining SARS compliance.
- Providing a clear supplier quotation.
- Responding quickly to requests for information.
- Ensuring your customer can verify the purchase order promptly.

Prepared businesses are usually funded much faster.
