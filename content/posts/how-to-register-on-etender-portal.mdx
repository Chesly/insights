# How to Register on the National eTender Portal

Registering on the Central Supplier Database (CSD) is only the first step.

To actually find government tender opportunities, your business should also use South Africa's National eTender Portal.

This portal publishes thousands of tenders from national departments, provincial governments, municipalities and public entities.

---

# What is the National eTender Portal?

The National eTender Portal is South Africa's official website for publishing government tenders.

Businesses use it to:

- Search open tenders
- Download tender documents
- View closing dates
- Read bid requirements
- Monitor procurement opportunities

---

# Do You Need a CSD Registration First?

While you can browse opportunities without being registered, having an active CSD profile prepares you to respond when you identify suitable tenders.

For this reason, registering on CSD should come before actively pursuing tenders.

---

# Step 1: Visit the National eTender Portal

Open the official portal using your web browser.

Review current tender listings.

Familiarise yourself with the different categories.

---

# Step 2: Search by Industry

Use the search filters to find tenders relevant to your business.

Examples include:

- Construction
- ICT
- Marketing
- Security
- Engineering
- Office Supplies
- Cleaning
- Professional Services

---

# Step 3: Download Tender Documents

Each opportunity includes documentation explaining:

- Scope of work
- Required documents
- Evaluation criteria
- Closing date
- Submission requirements

Read every page carefully.

---

# Step 4: Check Requirements

Before preparing a submission, ensure you have:

- Active CSD Registration
- Tax Compliance Status
- Company Profile
- B-BBEE Documentation (where applicable)
- Any industry-specific certificates

---

# Step 5: Prepare Your Submission

Never wait until the final day.

Allow enough time to:

- Gather supporting documents
- Complete forms
- Review pricing
- Verify signatures

---

# Tips for First-Time Tender Applicants

- Read every instruction.
- Never assume requirements.
- Submit before the deadline.
- Keep digital copies of everything.
- Ask clarification questions when allowed.

---

# Common Mistakes

Businesses often fail because they:

- Miss closing dates
- Submit incomplete documents
- Ignore mandatory requirements
- Price incorrectly
- Forget required signatures

Attention to detail is often the difference between success and rejection.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is the eTender Portal free?

Yes.

Browsing and downloading tenders is free.

---

## Can startups apply?

Yes.

Many tenders are open to qualifying small businesses, provided they meet the stated requirements.

---

## Can I search by province?

Yes.

The portal allows you to filter opportunities by location and issuing institution.
